Privacy Policy

Gallery · Brett Tech Networking · Last updated March 2026
The short version: Gallery never collects, uploads, or shares any of your personal data or photos. Everything stays on your device.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Gallery is an open-source, offline-first photo and video manager. We believe your memories are yours alone.

We have not built any analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or remote data collection into this app. All processing happens entirely on your device.

What We Do Not Collect

  • Your photos, videos, or any media files
  • Your name, email address, or any identifiers
  • Your location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics or crash reports sent externally
  • Any data transmitted to Brett Tech Networking servers

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The only data the app stores is your in-app preferences, saved entirely inside the app's private storage on your own device:

  • App theme and colour preferences
  • Album grid layout settings
  • Favourited items list
  • Secure Folder PIN (encrypted, device-only)
  • Album cover selections

This data is automatically erased when you clear the app's data or uninstall Gallery.

Permissions Explained

Gallery requests only the permissions it needs to function. None of these are used to transmit data off your device.

Permission Why it's needed
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES
READ_MEDIA_VIDEO
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE		 Read your photos and videos from device storage so the app can display them.
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Save edits, move files, and modify media on your device locally.
REQUEST_DELETE_PACKAGES Allow the app to delete or trash media items you choose to remove.
SET_WALLPAPER Set a selected photo as your device wallpaper when you request it.
USE_BIOMETRIC Lock the Secure Folder using your existing device biometrics. Biometric data is never read or stored by Gallery.

Third-Party Libraries

Gallery uses a small set of open-source Android libraries purely for local functionality:

  • Glide – image loading and caching (fully local)
  • uCrop – in-app image cropping (fully local)
  • Material Components – UI elements (fully local)

None of these libraries are configured to collect or transmit personal data.

Children's Privacy

Gallery does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no features that would gather personal information.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the new version will be included with the app update and the "last updated" date at the top will change. Continued use of Gallery after an update constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
